About Our Company

How We Got Started

In the spring of 1979, Lawnscape Systems rolled its first trucks into Southern California neighborhoods. With those trucks, we were the first professional lawn care company to open its doors in California’s Southland.

Unlike many of the “national” lawn care companies, we didn’t get here by moving across the country. Lawnscape Systems grew from its roots right here in Los Angeles County. We now service Southern California lawns from Ventura to the north, San Diego to the south, and San Bernardino to the east.
